Detailed Description

UnityJibeBridge is a class that works in the background as a bridge between Unity and the Jibe SDK on the Android side. It takes care of all of the instantiation of Jibe objects that a Unity programmer may need and signals Unity when callbacks occur.

Author
Marco Rizzo, Amir Ebrahimi Luminary Productions Inc.
